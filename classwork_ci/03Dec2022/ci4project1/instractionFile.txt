Codeigniter project Installation
1.E:\xampp\htdocs>composer create-project codeigniter4/appstarter ci4project1
2.E:\xampp\htdocs>cd ci4project1
3.E:\xampp\htdocs\cd ci4project1>php spark serve
4.if necessary update( composer update)
5. Make Controller:
	E:\xampp\htdocs\ci4project1>php spark make:controller pages
6. Make hello:
	E:\xampp\htdocs\ci4project1>php spark make:hello
7. Make Model:
	E:\xampp\htdocs\ci4project1>php spark make:model UserModel
8.Make usermodel controller:
	E:\xampp\htdocs\ci4project1>php spark make:controller Usercrud
